About Us

Our Company

Zeal Biotech Pvt Ltd is a privately-owned pharmaceuticals company founded by Mr Sachin Garg who has a rich experience of working in pharmaceutical marketing in India for over 16 years. The company was established in 2011 with the vision to provide high quality pharmaceutical products at affordable prices that enhances quality of life and delivers exceptional value to all our business associates. Driven by a will to succeed, Zeal Biotech, has, in a considerably short span of time, made its presence felt in the industry and has been successful in making a strong foothold in fiercely competitive market scenario through its unwavering focus on quality, commitment and delivery.

Why Choose Us?

Zeal Biotech has a comprehensive range of pharmaceutical formulations manufactured as per GMP norms and are being prescribed by the medical fraternity for treatment of various ailments. Our product portfolio covers all the major therapeutic segments and consist of Tablets, Dispersible Tablets, Capsules, Soft Gelatin Capsules, Sustained Released forms, Oral Liquids, External Liquids, Suspensions, Dry Syrups, Drops, Liquid Injections, Dry Powder Injections, Ointments, Creams, Gel, Lotions, Drops, Protein Powders etc..

Our products are widely appreciated for accurate composition and high standard of safety and efficiency.Our ethical approach and transparent business process have helped us in garnering a vast clientele within the domestic territory. We desire to expand our business all over India and are already operating our business in various states.
